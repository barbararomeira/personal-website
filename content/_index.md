# Hey, it's Bárbara! 👋

I'm an AI Product Leader with a PhD in Industrial Engineering — which is a long way of saying I spend my days asking what AI can actually *do* on a factory floor, not just what it looks like in a slide deck.

Right now I'm building the product function at an AI startup that puts computer vision into factories. Before that, I led continuous improvement programs at Hilti and Renault — basically helping operational teams across Europe and North America find what's broken and fix it. Somewhere along the way I picked up a PhD and a stack of Lean / Six Sigma / AI certifications.

Currently obsessed with: getting LLMs to do useful things humans can actually trust, the gap between "looks cool in a demo" and "ships to a customer," and how much manufacturing wisdom is sitting in plain sight on factory floors waiting to be productised.

I write here about what I'm learning, ship [side projects](/projects/), and occasionally share a strong opinion about product. Find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/barbararomeira), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-b%C3%A1rbara-gomes-romeira), or via the [full timeline](/experience/).
