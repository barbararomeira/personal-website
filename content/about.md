Hi, I'm Bárbara. I'm an AI Product Leader with a PhD in Industrial Engineering and 7+ years of experience across product management, lean manufacturing, and operational consultancy in Europe and North America.

I work where AI products meet the factory floor — translating frontline pain into shipped features for industrial enterprises.

## Currently

I'm the **AI Product Manager at Deltia.ai** (remote), where I founded the product function before the role formally existed and have helped scale the platform from 20 to 700+ deployed cameras (35x) across enterprise industrial clients in under two years.

My day-to-day is end-to-end product discovery — user interviews, JTBD synthesis, PRDs, wireframes, MVPs, and AI-native prototyping (Replit, Lovable, Claude Code). I currently lead an LLM-powered chat feature that lets customers build their own dashboards for their own use cases without engineering involvement. I co-own the roadmap and run the company-wide OKR process.

Alongside product, I built and lead Deltia's **Continuous Improvement & Customer Success** function from scratch — a team of 5 data scientists and CI managers deploying an AI-powered CI framework across 10+ client sites in automotive, power tools, luxury goods, and electrical systems. €3M+ in delivered cost savings, and a 30% increase in client conversion rates through consultative engagement.

The unusual part of this role is the feedback loop: direct customer engagement surfaces frontline pain points, which feed directly into the product roadmap and feature prioritization.
