Hi, I'm Bárbara — an AI Product Leader with a PhD in Industrial Engineering. I work where AI products meet the factory floor, translating frontline pain into shipped features for industrial enterprises.

Over the last 7+ years I've moved between product management, lean manufacturing, and operational consultancy across Europe and North America. Today I'm building the product function at an AI startup in industrial computer vision, while also leading customer success and continuous improvement engagements with enterprise clients.

I write here about what I'm learning — product discovery, AI in manufacturing, the messy middle between technical and operational teams — and I share side projects as they come together.
